Creamy Broccoli Vegan Pasta

Description:

CCreamy Broccoli Pasta is a pantry friendly recipe featuring a non-dairy cream sauce tossed with pasta and tender broccoli for a quick and easy vegan main dish ready in 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Brocolli
  • Flour
  • Nutritional Yeast
  • Plant milk
  • Garlic Powder
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt+pepper
  • Onion Powder

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta + broccoli. I like to cook my broccoli with the pasta. Just be sure you’ve added enough water to the pot if you do this. To do this I suggest adding the broccoli to the cooking pasta 3 minutes before pasta is done. Broccoli is at its best when it turns its brightest green. Set aside.
  2. Make the creamy base. You’ll create your creamy sauce by making a roux with flour, oil and non-dairy milk. The roux takes about 8 – 10 minutes to cook so you can start this while the water for the pasta is coming to a boil. Once done, cover and set aside.
  3. Assemble. Add your cream base to the pasta and broccoli, mix and serve. Season with salt + pepper.
